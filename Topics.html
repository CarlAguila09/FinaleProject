INTRODUCTION TO
HISTORICAL ANTECEDENTS

An antecedent is a preceding of the emergence or existence of anything. Hence, historical antecedents in science and technology have paved the way for the presence of advanced and sophisticated scientific and technical advancements today. Understanding the history of science and technology can help you evaluate today's advancements. Knowing how earlier generations affected and were influenced by advances in science and technology allows today's generation to make educated judgments about how science and technology should be applied in daily life.
